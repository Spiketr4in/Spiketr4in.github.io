    Introduzione

Create your identity

The first step to enter Idena Network consists in creating an account on the web app. Follow the steps below to be ready for the validation process.

Web app

1) Click on "Create an account"

Open the web app

When you open the web app, you will be asked to either create a new account or sign in if you already have one.

2) Click on "Proceed"

Create an account

Your identity has been generated, consisting of a public key (shown on screen) and a private key (to be kept safe, not shown on screen).

3) Use a strong password to export your private key

Export your private key

You will need to export your private key in an encrypted form: use a strong password and click on "next".

4) Store safely your private key and the password used to encrypt it.

Backup your private key

At this point your encrypted private key will pop up on screen; click on "copy" and store it safely with your password. Important: do not copy it just by taking a screenshot, as the key is longer than what shown on a single screen.
Optionally, copy your QR code as well, as an additional form to store your encrypted private key.
You will need the encrypted private key and the password to connect to the shared node. Click "Next" to finalize the process.

You are now ready for the next step
